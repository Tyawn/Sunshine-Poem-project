Sunshine Poem
~Terion Keemer

  • You were my sunshine
  • That always brightened up my day
  • Your beauty leaving me speechless
  • Even though its a lot I wanted to say
  • Without you it's dark
  • Like the clouds blocking the sun
  • With you I left my heart
  • That's Something that was never done
  • Going through the storm
  • I been feeling all alone
  • Missing your sunshine
  • One of the brightest things I've known
  • I hope one day the rain clear
  • And I can feel your warm rays once again
  • To the brightest star here
  • Know I'll love you until my end.
